FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly will I hear back?

I typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, feel free to send a DM on Instagram.

Do you offer in-person training?

Yes! I'm based in Morristown, NJ and offer in-person training locally. Remote/online coaching is also available worldwide.

What's included in the free consultation?

The free consultation is a no-pressure call where we discuss your goals, current fitness level, and which program might be the best fit for you. There's no obligation to purchase anything.

How do payments work?

Training plans are one-time payments. Private coaching is $500/month, billed once per month. All payments are processed securely and you can cancel private coaching at any time.

I'm a complete beginner. Is this right for me?

Absolutely. Programs are tailored to your current fitness level, whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced athlete. The free consultation helps me understand exactly where you're starting from.
